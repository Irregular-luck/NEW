INDIAN SOCIETY FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION (ISTE)

ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education) is a national-level professional organization that promotes technical education and skill development among students and educators. It conducts workshops, seminars, hackathons, and competitions to enhance knowledge in engineering, technology, and innovation. ISTE provides a platform for students to collaborate, develop leadership skills, and stay updated with industry trends.
