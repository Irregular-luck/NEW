Indian Society for Technical Education

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national-level organization committed to enhancing the quality of technical education in India. With a vast network of faculty and student members across the country, ISTE plays a key role in promoting research, skill development, and industry-academia interaction.

The ISTE Student Chapter at GECK is an active hub for innovation, organizing technical workshops, competitions, seminars, and hands-on training programs. It serves as a bridge between students and the professional world, equipping them with the latest technological skills, leadership qualities, and problem-solving abilities.

About the Host

Government Engineering College, Kozhikode (GECK) Established in 1999, Government Engineering College, Kozhikode (GECK) is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and technological innovation. Affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE, GECK offers cutting-edge undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. With a mission to nurture young minds and develop skilled professionals, GECK fosters a vibrant academic and research culture. The institution emphasizes practical learning, industry collaboration, and innovation, empowering students to excel in their respective fields. Over the years, GECK has built a strong reputation for producing technically sound, socially responsible, and globally competitive engineers.
