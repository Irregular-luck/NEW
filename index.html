Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national-level organization committed to enhancing the quality of technical education in India. With a vast network of faculty and student members across the country,ISTE plays a key role in promoting research, skill development, and industry-academia interaction. The major objective of the ISTE is to assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industries and other organizations
The ISTE Student Chapter at GECK is an active hub for innovation, organizing technical workshops, competitions, seminars, and hands-on training programs. The chapter serves as a bridge between students and the professional world, equipping them with the latest technological skills, leadership qualities, and problem-solving abilities.
