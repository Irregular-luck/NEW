Event Details

The 23rd Annual ISTE State Student Convention is a prestigious gathering of aspiring engineers and innovators from across Kerala. Hosted by Government Engineering College Kozhikode, this event serves as a platform to celebrate creativity, knowledge sharing, and technological excellence. With a variety of workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities, the convention fosters a spirit of collaboration and encourages students to explore emerging trends in engineering and technology. It is a unique occasion where ideas converge, talents shine, and participants leave inspired to make a meaningful impact in their fields.
